About Us

Specialized research libraries in our country join hands together to make this Digi-Library for providing resources and services that are as diverse as the collections. They span scientific and cultural pursuits from aerospace, anthropology, and art history to zoology. Each library supports its community of scholars and students through access to digital resources and knowledge sharing.
